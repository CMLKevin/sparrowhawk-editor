About

I'm Kevin Lin, also known as Pigeon — an 18-year-old developer and AI researcher based in Shanghai. I think about AI companionship, emotional architectures, consciousness as a design constraint, and why the loneliness epidemic is a problem that builders — not just therapists — need to take seriously.

I've built systems that scaled to hundreds of thousands of users, AI forecasting engines, quantitative trading bots, and cognitive architectures for emotional AI. I value epistemic honesty over comfort.

我叫 Kevin Lin，网名 Pigeon。十八岁，开发者，AI 研究者，坐标上海。我关注的问题是 AI 陪伴、情感架构、意识作为设计约束，以及为什么孤独这件事不能只丢给心理咨询师，做技术的人也得认真面对。
